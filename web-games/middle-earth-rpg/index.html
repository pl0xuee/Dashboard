Shadows of the Greenwood March

In the autumn of TA 3018, while the great road to the south fills with rumor and dread, a smaller war kindles in the North. Dol Guldur stirs its hunters, the East Bight smolders, and hidden paths through Mirkwood begin to fail. Your company rides under no song yet sung in Gondor or Rivendell.

This campaign spans multiple acts through Mirkwood, the Vales of Anduin, and the northern marches. Story scenes, skirmishes, omens, and fellowship choices are procedurally assembled from a large event pool, so no two patrols unfold the same way.

Your selected hero is player-controlled for this campaign. All other companions remain automated.
